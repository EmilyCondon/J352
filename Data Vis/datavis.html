2024 CFPB Complaints Against Navient Solutioins, LLC

by Emily R. Condon

In 2024, the Consumer Financial Protections Bureau, or CFPB, received 3083 complaints against Navient Solutions, LLC, according to publicly accessible data on the CFPB website.

The CFPB is a government agency that looks to provide consumer relief against complaints by consumers. The agency was created by legislation in 2011 championed by Sen. Elizabeth Warren (D-Mass.), and it has provided over $21 billion in consumer relief since 2011 when it began.
